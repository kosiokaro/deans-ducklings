Facts & Questions

Frequently asked questions

When are you available to meet? Office hours?

Answer. I am available almost every day, but check availability at the following site: https://soe.rutgers.edu/honorsacademyfaq. Walk-in sessions are also available pending if nobody else is meeting with me.

When do I have to decide my major?

Answer.Major declarations are sent out in the spring semester for engineering students and must be submitted shortly after.

I did not do too well in an honors class, what are my options?

Answer. It is advised that you come in and talk with me and we will figure out a solution.

I want to take more than 18 credit hours, do you think this a good option?

Answer.Run it by myself as well as your class advisor to make sure the credit load is appropriate.

What are some ways to become more involved on campus, with relation to my interests and major?

Answer.Ask around the Honors Academy to see what other people are involved in. There are many different and diverse clubs that will help extend your knowledge.
